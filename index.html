A World Class Multi-super Speciality Hospital


The Calcutta Medical Research Institute (CMRI) is one of the biggest multispecialty hospitals in Kolkata & is the flagship hospital of CK Birla Hospitals. For over 54 years this hospital has positioned itself as the preferred healthcare destination in Eastern India. Based on three key principles Clinical Excellence, Ethical Conduct and Patient Centricity this hospital has several eminent physicians, surgeons and health care providers of national and international repute. Apart from clinical excellence, patients experience care and empathy from internationally trained nurses which ensures holistic care for millions of patients who have come to us over the years. Centrally located in Kolkata with 440 beds and state-of-the-art infrastructure, accredited by the NABH, NABL and is recognized for its Quality Healthcare Service Delivery. CMRI has achieved a remarkable milestone of conducting over 5.5 lakh surgeries, showcasing its comprehensive range of medical, surgical, and critical care across multiple specialties, including transplant services. The institute is not only a center for patient care but also focuses on academic excellence by offering 1 FNB and 8 DNB courses for Post Graduate Medical Students, in addition to engaging in various clinical trials and research programs. Since 1970, CMRI has operated a Nursing School, contributing to the education and professional development of nursing staff.
